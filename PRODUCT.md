# Product

## Register

term-ptt-custom-theme

## Users

People who use `term.ptt.cc` and want terminal-style color and font control without leaving the browser. They are usually already familiar with PTT, ANSI colors, terminal palettes, and compact preference UIs.

## Product Purpose

Term PTT Custom Theme lets users preview, customize, and apply terminal color presets and font preferences for `term.ptt.cc`. Success means users can quickly scan many presets, understand what each palette will do to a terminal surface, preview safely, and apply only when they are satisfied.

## Brand Personality

Terminal-native, retro, compact, trustworthy.

## Anti-references

Avoid SaaS landing-page polish, oversized cards, decorative gradients, marketplace-style browsing, and UI that looks detached from terminal or PTT conventions. Avoid pure color-chip walls that do not explain terminal foreground/background behavior.

## Design Principles

- Make terminal behavior visible: show how foreground and background colors pair, not only what colors exist.
- Preserve density: the popup must stay fast to scan with hundreds of presets.
- Keep preview reversible: browsing and editing should feel safe until the user explicitly applies.
- Stay PTT-native: visual language can be retro and terminal-like without becoming noisy.
- Favor standard controls: use familiar selects, buttons, focus states, and browser-native affordances.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA for text and controls. Keep keyboard focus visible, avoid relying on color alone for selected or modified states, maintain readable contrast for light and dark terminal palettes, and respect reduced-motion preferences.
